Slacker Podcasts

We’ve got some great projects in the works, including an awesome podcast. Here are the podcasts we’ve worked on, newest first.

2020 Gaming Podcast (TBD)

Coming soon! We’re launching an all-new gaming podcast this year. Some familiar Slacker voices will be joined by new ones. Tune in to our feed for regular episodes. We’ll be announcing the release on our social media feeds, so be sure to check them out.

One Piece Panel

One Piece Panel — In 2017, a group of slackers came together to talk about their all-time favourite manga, One Piece. From comedic banter to insightful perpectives about the weekly releases, there’s a lot to take in. The show is currently on a hiatus but would likely return with fan support. The previous episodes can be found here.

BrownBoxCast

BrownBoxCast — From 2016-2017, the show featured hosts Farhan and Rahat who delved deep into news from the gaming world, with takes on the industry, economics, entertainment, and more. The show has been discontinued and will be replaced with a new gaming pod. You can listen to all the old episodes here.
