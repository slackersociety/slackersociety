      The Dream That Will Never Die: Dreamcast Independent Gaming in 2016

      2

EST 2011.

Believe it or not, we started up in 2011. Then titled the Intellectual Slacker Society, we mainly just tried new things, most of which we started and never got around to finishing. It started off with a few creative videos and some great insights into gaming, academia, and even psychology. It’s now our time to fulfill our legacy.

Mission

Why put it off until tomorrow when you can do it the day after tomorrow? Our goal is to share interesting perspectives from gaming, sports, entertainment, science, and culture. Promoting procrastination, one chill sesh at a time. For now, we’ve got a podcast and a blog. Stay tuned for some of the freshest content around.
